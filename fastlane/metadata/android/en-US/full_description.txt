FkCloud Share lets you send documents to a self-hosted rmfakecloud server
(the free reMarkable® cloud replacement, https://github.com/ddvk/rmfakecloud)
straight from the Android share sheet.

Features:

* Appears in the share menu for PDF and EPUB files (single or multiple)
* Pick the destination folder of your reMarkable library before uploading
* Works with any rmfakecloud instance over HTTPS (HTTP possible for LAN
  setups, but explicitly opt-in and discouraged)
* Credentials and session token are encrypted with a key stored in the
  Android Keystore (hardware-backed where available)
* No trackers, no analytics, no Google services — the app talks only to
  the server you configure

Setup: open the app once, enter your server URL and your rmfakecloud web
UI username and password, then share any PDF or EPUB from another app and
choose "FkCloud Share".
